Be Smart on the Deep Web

📌
Tip 1 – Avoid the Deep Web

It's always better to avoid problems than to fight them. Access only if necessary.

🔒
Tip 2 – Use a VPN

A VPN acts as a shield against hackers trying to get your IP.

🚫
Tip 3 – Block Notifications

Malicious sites send fake virus alerts and pop-ups. Avoid them.

💻
Tip 4 – Use a Virtual Machine

Always test suspicious files in a VM to avoid infecting your system.

🙅‍♂️
Tip 5 – Never Share Personal Info

Do not give your data to unknown or unsafe websites.
